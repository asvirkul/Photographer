About me

Hey there! I'm Dmytro Shulgan.

I'm a passionate Ukrainian photographer with a relentless drive to capture the essence of life through my lens. Armed with a Bachelor's degree in Photography from Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, I've turned my love for visuals into a journey of storytelling.

As a Freelance Photographer, I don't just take pictures; I create memories. Specializing in portrait, event, and commercial photography, I dive deep into each project, collaborating with clients to understand the heartbeat of their vision. My goal? To deliver images that not only meet expectations but surpass them.

My Services

Whether it's preserving cherished family moments, promoting a business through striking visuals, or adding an artistic flair to special events, I'm committed to delivering high-quality, meaningful photographs that tell a story.

Portrait Photography

My approach to portrait photography isn't just about snapping pictures. It's about unraveling stories, discovering the unique narratives that make each person extraordinary. I don't want to just capture faces; I want to capture the essence of who you are.

Event Photography

Your go-to guy for capturing the energy, excitement, and pure magic of your special moments. Event photography is not just a job for me; it's an opportunity to tell your unique story through my lens.

Commercial photography

if you're searching for a photographer who doesn't just take pictures but crafts visual stories to elevate your brand, you've found your match. Commercial photography isn't just about products; it's about creating a visual language that speaks to your audience

Nature and Landscape Photography

Nature and landscape photography aren't just about pretty pictures; they're about capturing the raw beauty of our planet and preserving it in every frame.

Fine Art Photography

Fine art photography is my canvas for expression. Through creative compositions, unique perspectives, and a blend of visual elements, I aim to create images that transcend the ordinary. It's about turning photography into an art form, one frame at a time.

Specialized Photography

I'm bringing a touch of uniqueness to my lens. Specialized photography is where I get to explore unconventional narratives, capturing moments that fall outside the usual categories
